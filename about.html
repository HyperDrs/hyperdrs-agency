About DRS

Running-store marketing specialists

DRS combines local-search strategy, advertising, content, community marketing, and retention systems for independent running shoe retailers.

Why DRS exists

Independent running stores offer expertise, product knowledge, fittings, and community relationships that large general retailers cannot easily replicate. DRS exists to help those strengths become visible online and measurable in-store.

Our work is designed for single-location owners, growing family businesses, and multi-location operators that want clearer reporting and a repeatable marketing system.
